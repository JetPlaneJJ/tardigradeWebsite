While tardigrades may seem scary or weird to many people, they actually appear in more popular media than people think. Tardigrades have been featured in tv shows and the news. A small fanbase creates home-made tardigrade dolls, stickers, cartoon drawings, and even a song. Song can be found here: youtube.com
Below is a table containing examples of the following media in which tardigrades have appeared.

Tardigrades in Media

Name Genre Date(s) of Appearance Notes
Cosmos: A Spacetime Odyssey Nonfiction TV Show March 16, 2014 & April 13, 2014 In episode 2, astrophysicist Neil deGrasse Tyson describes how tardigrades were able to withstand five great extinction events and still thrive today. In episode 6, Tyson describes micro-organisms that live within a dew drop, demonstrating parameciums and tardigrades.
Tardigrades Adventure Game by AnasAbdin Video Game Mid 2015 Tardigrades is a PC single-player adventure video game that prominently features tardigrades. It was released on Steam.
If Tardigrades wore pants... Internet Meme 2016-current Source: knowyourmeme.com
Star Trek: Discovery Sci-Fi TV Show October 1, 2017 In "Context is for Kings", the third episode of the space odyssey American TV series, an unknown creature annihilates the USS Glenn space ship. The Tardigrade (presented as a large alien species) is later introduced as the perpetrator of the incident.
Family Guy Comedy TV Show October 21, 2018 Stewie invents a shrinking machine that results in his son and him shrinking to a microscopic size. They meet a group of friendly tardigrades who help them.
